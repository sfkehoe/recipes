250g wholemeal flour
100g plain white flour
50g porridge oats
1tsp bread soda
1tsp salt
3tsp sugar
1 egg
250ml buttermilk

- preheat oven to 200C
- mix egg and buttermilk
- mix dry ingredients
- rub butter into dry ingredients
- knead wet ingredients into dry until sticky
- lightly knead dough
- shape, cut, and bake for 20 minutes until brown
- cover with foil and bake for further 20 minutes
